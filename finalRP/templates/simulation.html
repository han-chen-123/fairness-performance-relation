Golden Eagles is a technology company operating a wide range of businesses. With the company expansion, the demand for employees is significantly increasing. To shortlist suitable candidates effectively and efficiently, the company developed an Automatic Recruiting Assistant (ARA) to solve the dilemma. ARA uses historical recruiting data to train the model and make a prediction to help the decision-making process. Since every machine learning model has errors, the model performance becomes crucial. This survey aims to investigate the satisfaction rate based on different model performance.
